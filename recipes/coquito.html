coquito recipe

Description

Coquito is a creamy, tropical, Puerto Rican holiday drink made with both evaporated and condensed milk, cream of coconut, spices, and rum.

Ingredients

  • evaporated milk
  • eggs
  • cream of coconut
  • sweetened condensed milk
  • white rum
  • water
  • vanilla
  • spices

Steps

  1. cook the evaporated milk and egg yolks in a double boiler until thick
  2. transfer the mixture to a blender and blend with the remaining ingredients
  3. transfer to glass bottles and refrigerator until cold
